Budget-Friendly Diet for Donors

A simple, low-cost guide to prepare your body before donation and recover quickly afterwards using everyday Indian staples.

Preparation

Before Donation

Hydration
Plain Water (2-3 glasses) Lemon Water (Nimbu Pani) Buttermilk (Chaas)
Iron-Rich Foods (Cheap & Best)
Spinach (Palak) Methi Leaves Jaggery (Gul) + Chana Poha with Peanuts Dal (Toor/Moong)
Vitamin C (Absorb Iron)
Squeeze Lemon on Dal Guava (Peru) Amla Oranges
Pro Tip: Avoid Tea or Coffee just before donation as they reduce iron absorption in the body.
Recovery

After Donation

Immediate Snack
Banana / Apple Biscuits Mosambi/Sugarcane Juice
Replenish Fluids
Coconut Water ORS Packets Plenty of Water
Protein & Recovery
Eggs (Boiled) Sprouts (Moong/Matki) Rajma/Chole Rice Curd (Dahi) Dates (Khajoor)
Rest: Avoid heavy exercise or lifting weights for at least 24 hours after donating.

Why This Works?

This plan uses everyday Indian staples available in Pune markets.

🥬 Spinach & Jaggery Provides Iron for Hemoglobin
🍋 Lemon & Amla Vitamin C for Iron Absorption
🥚 Eggs & Curd Protein for Muscle Recovery
🥥 Coconut Water Quick Energy & Hydration

Things to Avoid

Strictly avoid these to ensure a safe donation experience.

Alcohol (24hrs)
Smoking
Heavy Exercise
Skipping Meals
